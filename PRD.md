# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Website Development – Advanced Power And Infra Solutions

---

# 1. Product Overview

The project involves designing and developing a modern, professional website for **Advanced Power And Infra Solutions**, a company specializing in diesel generator solutions.

The website will act as a digital platform to showcase the company’s services, highlight completed projects, and generate business enquiries.

The platform will primarily focus on generating leads for:

- Diesel Generator Rental
- Diesel Generator Sales
- Used Generator Buyback
- Generator AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts)
- Generator Service and Maintenance

The website should reflect reliability, technical expertise, and the company's experience in delivering power solutions.
